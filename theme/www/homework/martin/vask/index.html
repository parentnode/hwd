Technology needs humanity

Vice versa.

  • 2016-06-12, 00:02
  • Martin Kæstel Nielsen
    • parentnode.dk

We are a web agency with a holistic approach to development. That means there is more to it for us than just work. We actually like making the world a better place through programming and we think success is about more than meeting the deadline. When we do projects we want everyone involved in the process to learn something new. That is how we can all grow, personally or professionally, together – even while at work.

We are proud to deliver long lasting, easily maintainable, search engine optimized, energy efficient and fully backwards compatible web sites, services and applications of any scale. From award winning, animated experiences to learning platforms, shopping- and distribution systems – or any other combination of all these fancy words.

We favor clients with regenerative purposes or who otherwise (also) serve the common good. When we all give more, we all get more and we believe humans thrive better in a sustainable ecosystem of abundance.
